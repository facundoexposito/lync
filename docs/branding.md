## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Display/Headings | **Mogena** | Bold | Page titles, hero headings, section headers |
| Body/UI | **Avenir** | Regular/Medium | Body text, navigation, buttons, form labels |
| Fallback | Sans-serif | — | System fallback |

**Mogena** is a decorative serif/display font used for all large headings. Gives a warm, editorial, slightly feminine feel.

**Avenir** is the workhorse — clean, modern, highly readable.

## Tone of Voice

Rebecca's copy is the brand's strongest asset. Key characteristics:

- **Warm & personal:** "Hey! We're Rebecca and Cordelia"
- **Emotionally honest:** "We spent months feeling totally lost, isolated"
- **Gen Z friendly:** casual, relatable, not corporate
- **Community-first:** "you won't stay alone for long"
- **Reassuring:** addresses fears directly (coming alone, age, what to expect)

### Copy examples worth preserving:
- "strangers turn into your new favorite people"
- "that comforting 'I belong here' feeling"
- "new places, new memories, and new friendships waiting to happen"
- "your little guide to life, connection, and joy in Madrid"

### CTAs style:
- Inviting, not transactional
- "Find your people" > "Sign up"
- "Join the Community" > "Subscribe"

## Visual Language

- **Photography style:** Real event photos > stock. Authentic moments of women connecting, laughing, socializing. Warm indoor lighting, candid shots.
- **Layout:** Full-width hero sections, card-based content, generous whitespace
- **Animations:** fadeIn, fadeInUp, zoomIn (subtle Elementor transitions)
- **Rounded corners:** On cards and testimonial blocks
- **Icons:** Blue line icons for feature sections (safety, friends, events, international)

## Social Proof Elements
